HOW TO PLAY: Begin the game by pressing 'START GAME'. To attempt a word search, click or tap the first letter of the word, then the last letter.

SCORING:Words searched in the first minute of play yield 10 points each. That decreases to 5 in the second minute, 3 in the third, 2 in the fourth and 1 every minute thereafter. A bonus of 50 points is awarded for completing the puzzle in the first minute, 25 in the second, 10 in the third and five thereafter.

Careful though, an unsuccessful word search will subtract a point from your score!
